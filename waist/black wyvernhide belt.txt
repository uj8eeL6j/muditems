black wyvernhide belt
Rigid, yet flexible, dyed black hide of a wyvern is the basis material of this belt. Surface of the hide is evenly covered in rectangular crystalline plates of azure shades, leaving spaces for fastening holes. Buckle of the belt depict symbolic eye and is casted out of palladium. The belt is rather long, with palladium incased end swinging on the side.
