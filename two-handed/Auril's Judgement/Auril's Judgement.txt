double-edged mithril-handled translucent ice axe
Blade of this double-edged axe is forged out of imbued with power ice. It is formed as sharp two-crescent solid piece ornamented with thin pattern of decorative snowflakes at the surface, with mithril handle binding it. Handle ends with a long spike, and a hook for hanging on the other end, is bound by leather stripes. Axe itself is surrounded by aura of cold, so dense at the blade so snowflakes form in the air around it.
