crystalline lance with redwood pole
Body of this long shimmering lance is forged out of azure crystalline matter into perfectly smooth, tall cone. Suited for piercing shape has no imperfections and is nearly transparent on a glance. Guard for the hand is extended from the end of the body and hides the hand inside. Polished pole of the lance is carved out of quality redwood and is long enough for the weapon to be held against the elbow. Butt of the tool is fashioned as a spere of the same crystalline matter. Lance is balanced to have center of weight just inside the guard.
